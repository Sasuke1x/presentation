Theme 1

AI is powerful, but it is not magic

What it does well

  • Generates options quickly
  • Summarizes complex input
  • Builds first drafts at scale

Where it breaks

  • Hallucinates details
  • Can sound confident while wrong
  • Misses business context without guidance
Speed without judgment creates rework
Output quality depends on context quality
Human ownership remains non-negotiable
Theme 2

The 16-year-old genius analogy

It can do almost anything fast, but it also makes things up and follows bad instructions perfectly.

Your job is to be the adult in the room.

How to manage it well

  • Set clear task boundaries
  • Provide relevant context and constraints
  • Demand evidence for factual claims
  • Review before anything ships
